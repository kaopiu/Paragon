Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Will the screen match my car's existing interior?

Every screen we install is precision-engineered for your specific make, model, and year. We use OEM-grade mounting brackets and custom trim pieces, so the result is indistinguishable from a factory installation — including matching leather, wood, or carbon fibre trim.

How long does the installation take?

Most installations are completed within a single day — typically 4 to 6 hours. Drop your vehicle off in the morning and pick it up the same afternoon, fully configured and ready to drive.

Do your screens support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes. All our screen systems fully support wireless Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and screen mirroring. We configure everything before you pick up the vehicle, so it's ready to use the moment you drive away.

Which vehicle brands do you work with?

We service all major luxury and premium brands including BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Lexus, Toyota, Hyundai, Kia, and more. Contact us with your specific model to confirm compatibility — we cover most vehicles from 2015 onwards.
